Intellectual property law can be complicated, and we do not want to end up in a situation where our own repositories contain IP that we do not have the rights to use or distribute. In order to make things clear, we ask all open source contributors to sign this CLA.

When you open your first pull request against one of Neocrym's projects on GitHub, the CLA Assistant bot will reply asking you to sign the below CLA.

Neocrym's CLA is adapted from the Google Individual Contributor License Agreement.
